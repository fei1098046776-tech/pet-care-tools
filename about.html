About PetCalc

We are a team of passionate pet lovers and data nerds who realized that finding accurate, fast, and free calculators for our furry friends was harder than it should be.

Our mission is simple: To provide completely free, vet-standard calculators to keep your pets safe and healthy.

Whether you're panicking because your dog ate a chocolate bar, or you just want to know exactly how much to feed your new kitten, PetCalc is here to give you instant peace of mind.
