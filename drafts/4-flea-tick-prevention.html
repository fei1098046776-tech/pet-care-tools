The Ultimate Guide to Flea and Tick Prevention

Category: Preventative Care • April 2026

Why Prevention is Crucial

Fleas and ticks are more than just a nuisance; they carry dangerous diseases like Lyme disease and tapeworms. A single tick bite can lead to lifelong joint issues for your dog.

Keep Your Pet Healthy

Ensure your pet's diet is perfectly balanced.

Topical vs. Oral Preventatives

Topical treatments are applied to the back of the neck and kill parasites on contact. Oral chews circulate in the bloodstream, meaning the parasite must bite to die, but they are often more effective long-term.

When to Start

Puppies should begin preventatives around 8 weeks of age. Always consult your vet to choose the safest option based on your dog's weight and breed.
