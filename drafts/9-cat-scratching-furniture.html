How to Stop Your Cat from Scratching Furniture

Category: Behavior • April 2026

Scratching is a Biological Need

Cats do not scratch to destroy your couch. They scratch to shed the dead outer layers of their claws, mark their territory, and stretch their back muscles.

Keep Your Pet Healthy

Ensure your pet's diet is perfectly balanced.

Use Calorie Calculator

Provide the Right Alternatives

If your cat scratches the side of the sofa, they want a tall, sturdy vertical scratching post. If they scratch the carpet, provide a horizontal cardboard scratcher.

Deterrents and Rewards

Apply double-sided sticky tape to the furniture corners. When they use the correct scratching post, immediately reward them with high-value treats and praise.
