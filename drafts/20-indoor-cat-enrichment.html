10 Indoor Cat Enrichment Ideas to Keep Your Cat Happy

Published: May 20, 2026

Bored cats develop behavioral problems. Enrichment keeps them thriving.

1. Window Perches with Bird Feeders

Hours of natural entertainment every day.

2. Puzzle Feeders

Make your cat work for their food — stimulates problem-solving.

3. Cat Trees

A multi-level cat tree near a window is one of the best investments.

4. Rotate Toys Weekly

Cats get bored with the same toys. Rotate every 5-7 days.

5. Daily Wand Play

Even 10 minutes of active play burns energy and reduces stress.
