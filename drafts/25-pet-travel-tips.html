How to Travel With Your Pet: Car and Plane Tips

Published: May 30, 2026

Traveling with a pet requires planning but is entirely doable.

Car Travel

Never let your dog ride unrestrained. Use a crash-tested harness or crate.

Stop every 2-3 hours for water and bathroom breaks.

Air Travel

Each airline has different pet policies. Check at booking, not at the gate.

Never sedate without vet guidance — sedation at altitude can be dangerous.
