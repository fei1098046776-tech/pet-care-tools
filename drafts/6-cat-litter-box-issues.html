Why is My Cat Peeing Outside the Litter Box?

Category: Cat Health • April 2026

Rule Out Medical Issues First

Before assuming it's a behavioral problem, visit the vet. Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs) or kidney crystals cause immense pain, making cats associate the litter box with hurting.

Keep Your Pet Healthy

Ensure your pet's diet is perfectly balanced.

Use Calorie Calculator

The N+1 Rule

If you have one cat, you need two litter boxes. If you have two cats, you need three. Cats are territorial and prefer having multiple pristine options.

Location Matters

Never place the litter box next to loud appliances like washing machines or next to their food bowl. Cats prefer quiet, safe areas with multiple escape routes.
