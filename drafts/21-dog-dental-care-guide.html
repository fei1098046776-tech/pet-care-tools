Dog Dental Care: How to Prevent Costly Vet Bills

Published: May 22, 2026

Dental disease affects 80% of dogs over age 3 — almost entirely preventable.

Daily Brushing

Use dog toothpaste (never human toothpaste — xylitol is toxic). Focus on outer surfaces where tartar builds.

Dental Chews

Look for the VOHC seal — clinically proven to reduce plaque.

Water Additives

Work passively with no cooperation needed from your dog.

Annual Professional Cleanings

Budget $300-800 depending on location and dog size.
