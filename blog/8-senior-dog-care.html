How to Care for a Senior Dog: Diet and Joint Health

Category: Health Insights • April 2026

When is a Dog Considered Senior?

Giant breeds are seniors by age 6, while small breeds reach senior status around age 10. You will notice increased sleeping and a graying muzzle.

Keep Your Pet Healthy

Ensure your pet's diet is perfectly balanced.

Use Calorie Calculator

Joint Supplements

Arthritis affects 80% of senior dogs. Supplements containing Glucosamine, Chondroitin, and Omega-3 fatty acids can dramatically improve their mobility and comfort.

Adjusting Their Diet

Senior dogs have slower metabolisms and require fewer calories but higher quality, easily digestible protein to maintain muscle mass without gaining weight.
