How to Introduce a New Cat to Your Home

Published: May 26, 2026

Cats are territorial. A rushed introduction leads to fighting and lasting tension.

Phase 1: Separate Rooms (Days 1-7)

Keep new cat isolated with their own food, water, and litter.

Phase 2: Scent Swapping (Days 3-7)

Swap bedding so cats get used to each other's scent first.

Phase 3: Visual Introduction (Week 2)

Use a baby gate. Feed both cats near the barrier.

Phase 4: Supervised Interaction (Week 3+)

Allow brief meetups. Redirect with toys if tension rises.
