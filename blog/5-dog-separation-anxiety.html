5 Proven Ways to Cure Dog Separation Anxiety

Category: Behavior • April 2026

Recognizing the Signs

Destructive chewing, excessive barking, and indoor accidents when you leave are classic signs of separation anxiety, not spite or bad behavior.

Keep Your Pet Healthy

Ensure your pet's diet is perfectly balanced.

Use Calorie Calculator

Desensitizing Departure Cues

Dogs learn that grabbing your keys means you are leaving. Practice picking up your keys and sitting back down until your dog no longer reacts with panic.

High-Value Distractions

Give your dog a frozen Kong stuffed with peanut butter right before you leave. This redirects their anxiety into a rewarding puzzle-solving task.
