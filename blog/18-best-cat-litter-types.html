Best Cat Litter Types: Clumping vs Non-Clumping vs Silica

Published: May 16, 2026

Choosing the right cat litter affects odor control and your cleaning routine.

Clumping Clay

Best for most households. Easy to scoop, great odor control, but heavy and dusty.

Non-Clumping Clay

Very affordable. Best for kittens under 3 months. Requires full box change weekly.

Silica Crystal

Lasts up to 30 days for one cat. Very low dust. More expensive but low maintenance.

Natural Options

Pine, paper, corn, and wheat litters are eco-friendly. Cost more but biodegradable.
