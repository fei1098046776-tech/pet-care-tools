How to Bathe Your Dog at Home Without the Struggle

Published: May 14, 2026

Bathing your dog at home can be a bonding experience with the right preparation.

What You'll Need

  • Dog-safe shampoo
  • Non-slip mat
  • Towels
  • Handheld sprayer

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Brush First

Always brush before bathing to remove loose fur and prevent matting.

2. Use Lukewarm Water

Test on your wrist — too hot causes anxiety.

3. Apply Shampoo and Rinse Completely

Leftover shampoo causes skin irritation. Rinse until water runs clear.

How Often?

Most dogs need a bath every 4-6 weeks. Over-bathing strips natural oils.
