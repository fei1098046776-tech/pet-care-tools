The Truth About Raw Diets for Dogs: Pros and Cons

Category: Diet • April 2026

What is a Raw Diet?

A raw diet consists of uncooked muscle meat, organ meat, and crushed bones. Proponents claim it leads to shinier coats, cleaner teeth, and smaller stools.

The Risks of Raw Feeding

The FDA and AVMA strongly oppose raw diets due to the high risk of Salmonella and E. coli contamination, which poses a severe threat to both the dog and the human handling the food.

Balancing the Nutrients

Creating a nutritionally complete raw diet at home is incredibly difficult. Calcium and phosphorus imbalances in homemade raw diets frequently lead to severe bone deformities in growing dogs.
