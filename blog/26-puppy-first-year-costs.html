True Cost of Owning a Puppy in the First Year

Published: June 1, 2026

The first year is the most expensive. Here's an honest breakdown.

Adoption or Purchase

Rescue: $50-500 | Breeder: $800-3,000+

Vet and Vaccines

$200-400 for first checkup plus puppy vaccine series.

Spay or Neuter

$200-600 depending on size and location.

Food (Annual)

$360-960 for quality puppy food.

First Year Total

$2,500 - $6,000+ depending on breed, location, and emergencies.
