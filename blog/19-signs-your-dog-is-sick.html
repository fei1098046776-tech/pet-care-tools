10 Warning Signs Your Dog May Be Sick

Published: May 18, 2026

Dogs can't tell you when they're sick, but their bodies give clear signals.

1. Loss of Appetite Over 24 Hours

Two missed meals is a red flag, especially with lethargy.

2. Excessive Thirst

Can signal diabetes or kidney disease.

3. Vomiting More Than Twice Daily

Repeated vomiting with blood requires immediate vet attention.

4. Difficulty Breathing

Blue gums is an emergency — go to the vet immediately.

5. Swollen Abdomen

Bloat (GDV) is life-threatening. A distended stomach with failed vomiting attempts is an emergency.
