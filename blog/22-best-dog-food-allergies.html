Best Dog Food for Allergies: What to Look For

Published: May 24, 2026

Food allergies are more common than most owners realize. Symptoms include itchy skin and ear infections.

Common Allergens

  • Beef (most common)
  • Dairy
  • Chicken
  • Wheat

How to Identify

Elimination diet: feed only a novel protein for 8-12 weeks, then reintroduce one at a time.

What to Look For

  • Novel proteins: venison, rabbit, duck
  • Limited ingredients
