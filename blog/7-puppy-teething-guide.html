Surviving Puppy Teething: Timeline and Tips

Category: Puppy Care • April 2026

The Teething Timeline

Puppies begin losing their baby teeth around 12 to 16 weeks of age. By 6 months, all 42 adult teeth should be fully erupted.

Keep Your Pet Healthy

Ensure your pet's diet is perfectly balanced.

Use Calorie Calculator

Managing the Biting

During this phase, their gums hurt constantly. Redirect all nipping to appropriate chew toys. Never use your hands to play wrestle with a teething puppy.

Frozen Relief

Wet a washcloth, twist it into a rope, and freeze it. Letting your puppy chew on the frozen cloth numbs their gums and provides hours of safe relief.
