Best Small Dog Breeds for Apartment Living

Published: May 28, 2026

Not all small dogs are quiet or low-maintenance. The right breed depends on your lifestyle.

French Bulldog

Low energy, calm, affectionate. Doesn't need much exercise. Perfect apartment dog.

Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

Gentle and adaptable. Great with kids and seniors.

Shih Tzu

Happy with short daily walks. Requires regular grooming.

What to Avoid

Beagles and Jack Russells are small but high-energy and vocal — not ideal for apartments.
