Why is Chocolate Bad for Dogs?

Chocolate contains theobromine and caffeine (both classified as methylxanthines). Dogs metabolize these compounds much slower than humans, leading to a build-up of toxins. Symptoms of toxicity include vomiting, diarrhea, rapid breathing, increased heart rate, and in severe cases, seizures or heart failure. If your dog is showing symptoms, seek emergency veterinary care immediately regardless of this calculator's result.
