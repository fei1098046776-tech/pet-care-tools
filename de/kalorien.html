How Do We Calculate This?

We use the standard veterinary formula. First, we calculate the Resting Energy Requirement (RER): 70 × (Body Weight in kg)^0.75. Then, we multiply the RER by a multiplier based on your pet's life stage and activity level (Daily Energy Requirement - DER). This gives you the exact kcal per day to keep your furry friend at an optimal weight!
